About Me

I am a software engineer ready to make lives easier and simpler by crafting solutions to problems and frustrations. I am currently earning a certificate in full stack development from The Ohio State University Coding Bootcamp. I have recently developed my skills with JavaScript, CSS, HTML, Node, Express, and MySQL. I recently worked on a team of six to develop an app that helps prospective dog owners find a dog in their area that is available for adoption and matches their wish list of traits. During this project, I used agile development, server-side APIs, and third-party APIs. My aim is to use industry best practices to build quality applications that solve pain points whether they are nearly universal or extremely niche. I am excited to use my recently acquired software engineering skills and my life-long passion for creating to work collaboratively with a team who makes a difference for people.
